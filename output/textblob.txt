Hospitals make NNY economy healthier

BYLINE: Alex Jacobs, Watertown Daily Times, N.Y.

2:	Recent economic impact
reports by the Healthcare Association of New York State show just how far that
money goes.

3:	Using economic modeling software developed by the U.S. Department of Commerce's
Bureau of Economic Analysis, HANYS estimates each hospital's economic impact
using multipliers to show the ripple effect each dollar a medical center spends
has on its community.

4:	According to HANYS's Economic and Community Benefit Report, Samaritan Medical
Center provided 2,090 jobs in 2006, with a payroll of $143,043,000.

5:	All in all, Samaritan's total estimated economic impact was $238,728,000 in
2006.

6:	At Claxton-Hepburn Medical Center, employee dollars go a long way in the
Ogdensburg economy -- and the hospital helps the city attract businesses.

7:	Dollars earned by hospital employees generated approximately $73,336,000
in economic activity for the Ogdensburg area.

8:	The hospital's total estimated
economic impact in 2006 was $133,499,000.

9:	"It's a very large economic engine in the community.

10:	"Many people, when they're choosing a college or job, look to access to health
care as a key part of where they go," said Richard D. Jacobs, the hospital's
vice president for finance.

11:	The hospital had a payroll of $29,978,000 for its 560 employees in 2006, which
accounted for another 1,010 jobs in the community and generated approximately
$57,882,000 for local businesses.

12:	Canton-Potsdam Hospital's overall economic
impact was estimated to be $108,987,000 in 2006.

13:	Lewis County General Hospital and Residential Health Care Facility, Lowville,
with a payroll of $23,132,000, provided 500 jobs and helped to generate
approximately $44,663,000 in the Lowville area in 2006, according to the report.

14:	The hospital spent $10,567,000 on goods and services and $3,537,000 on buildings
and equipment; its total estimated economic impact was $71,895,000.

15:	Carthage Area Hospital's payroll was $31,090,000 in 2006, and it provided 540
jobs.

16:	The hospital's estimated total economic impact was
$50,640,000.
